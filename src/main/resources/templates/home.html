Getting Started
What is Virtual Wallet?

Virtual Wallet is the platform to end all your e-pay and money transfer problems. You can send money to your mother or pay for that much needed pair of latex pants. We don't judge.

How to start using Virtual Wallet?

The Virtual Wallet app is easy to use. You just verify your e-mail, add a Bank Card, TopUp your wallet and start making transactions with the billions of other customers. Easy as 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 ... you get the point.

Where to add a Bank Card?

In order to start using our services, you first need to add a Bank Card. This can be done from the Bank Card tab at the top of the screen.

Are my moneys safe?

Virtual Wallet is a unique platform that is secure, smart and easy-to-use. This being said, we checked with Ivan in storage and funds are safe.
